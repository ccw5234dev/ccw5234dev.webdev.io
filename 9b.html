You wake up the next morning, and see that there are other couriers around you,
along with what looks like a ferryman offering to take the couriers across.
Right before everyone gets on the boat, he warns everyone,
   "Beware of the Anx, they are spirits that use the river as one of their feeding grounds.
   They hunt by weakening their prey through the calls that they make."


You get on the boat with the others.

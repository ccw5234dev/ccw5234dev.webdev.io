You start speculating about what the blobs were.
They looked and felt different from sweat, what could they have been?
An itch inside your nose swells, causing you to sneeze into your arm. You wipe your nose with the back of your hand and continue walking,
but then 2 questions pop into your head,
   “Why didn’t I feel my arm feel sneezing into it, and why did I not feel my hand against my face while wiping my nose?”
You examine your arm to see blotches of the translucent blobs all over it.
You immediately stop and start to inspect your whole body and find the translucent blobs scattered throughout your limbs and back.

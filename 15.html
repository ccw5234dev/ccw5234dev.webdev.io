Right as everyone was leaving or setting up camp,
Ferryman Charn calls out to everyone saying,
    "To those who plan on going into the Spirit Forest,
     it is home to many spirits including the Anxes,
     you must be vigilant and avoid interacting with the spirits or else they'll consume you."

You recover for the night, and wake up well rested.

Keeping the ferryman's warning in mind you begin your journey into the Spirit Forest.

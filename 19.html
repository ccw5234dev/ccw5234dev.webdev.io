You continue to make your way up the mountain in the Spirit Forest,
you wished there was an easier way, but this is the only way you know.
Suddenly you hear a noise behind you. You look back and spot a couple couriers proceeding up the path as well.
You watch silently as they pass by with determined and experienced faces.
A little feeling in your stomach starts to bubble up, you begin to worry if you’ll ever get to be like them.

Thoughts of doubt and insecurity start forming. You want to greet them and ask for advice to get through the forest,
but they’re too busy on their own mission to help a rookie out. All you can do is watch in silence as they go out of sight.

You casually say,
   “Are you going to Suss Fal as well?”
They light up and nod endearingly with a smile.
   “Yeah, I'm just resting and eating, I don’t know what to expect at Suss Fal base”
While laughing at their sincerity, a rumbling sound emerged out of nowhere and you look down at your stomach to see that it’s the cause.
The courier laughs and says,
   “Here, have some of my food, I am full anyway”
   “Thank you”, you happily reply while taking their food and quickly finishing it.
After the courier was finished cleaning up, you both stood up and walked to Suss Fal base together regailing your journeys to each other.
